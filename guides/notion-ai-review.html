What Is Notion AI?

Notion AI is an AI assistant built into Notion, the popular all-in-one workspace tool. Instead of switching between Notion and ChatGPT, you get AI capabilities directly in your workspace.

Notion itself is a flexible tool for notes, databases, wikis, project management, and more. The AI addition brings writing assistance, summarization, and generation capabilities.

I've been a Notion user for years. Let me share how the AI integration actually affects my workflow.

Writing Assistance

Notion AI offers several writing-focused features:

Continue Writing

Start a paragraph, click "Continue writing," and AI completes your thought. This works surprisingly well for generating rough drafts that you then refine. I used it for expanding meeting notes into full documentation.

Rewrite

Select text and ask AI to rewrite it. Options include:

  • Make it longer or shorter
  • Change tone (formal, casual, simple)
  • Fix spelling and grammar
  • Simplify language

The rewrite quality is decent—not as sophisticated as Grammarly for grammar, but integrated into the writing experience.

Draft from Outline

Create a document outline, ask AI to "Write a draft based on outline," and get full content. This is useful for structured documents like reports or project plans.

I tested this for a project proposal. Gave an outline of sections, AI generated paragraphs for each. The output was generic but usable—a solid first draft to refine.

Summarization and Extraction

This is Notion AI's genuinely useful feature. You can:

Summarize

Select a long document, click "Summarize," get key points. I used this for condensing meeting transcripts and lengthy articles. The summaries are accurate and capture main points.

Extract Action Items

From meeting notes, AI can identify action items and create task items. This is a real workflow improvement if you take notes in Notion.

Find Action Items from Text

Similar to extraction, but for identifying what needs to be done. Works best with structured text (meeting notes, project updates) rather than narrative content.

Brainstorming Features

Brainstorm

Enter a topic, get a list of ideas. Not revolutionary—you could do this with any AI—but integrated into your workspace. Useful for quick ideation sessions.

Outline from Text

Upload a document, get an outline structure. Good for organizing existing content into clearer structure.

Blog Post Ideas

Dedicated brainstorm feature for content ideation. Enter a topic, get post ideas. The quality is comparable to general AI tools.

Notion Workflow Integration

Notion AI's value comes from being embedded in Notion's workflow. If you're already using Notion for:

  • Meeting notes
  • Documentation
  • Project wikis
  • Personal knowledge management

...then AI adds genuine value by staying in context. No switching tabs, no copy-pasting.

If you're not a Notion user, the AI features alone aren't compelling enough to adopt the entire platform. Notion is a powerful but opinionated tool—you should use it because you like the workspace features, not because of AI.

Pricing Breakdown

Notion Plus at $12/month includes unlimited AI access. This is reasonable if you're already using Notion and find the AI features helpful. The upgrade from Free to Plus is worth it for regular users.

Final Verdict

Notion AI is a solid addition to an already useful tool. It's not revolutionary AI, but the integration into Notion's workflow makes it practical.

If you're already a Notion power user, the Plus subscription with AI is worth it. The summarization and writing assistance save time in your existing workflow.

If you're not using Notion, this isn't a reason to switch. Notion requires adopting its workflow, and the AI alone doesn't justify that adoption.

My rating: 8.0/10. Good AI integration for the right use case.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Notion AI free?

Notion has a free tier with limited AI uses (about 20 AI commands). The Plus plan at $12/month includes unlimited AI access.

Is Notion AI better than ChatGPT?

Not exactly comparable. Notion AI is integrated into Notion's workflow; ChatGPT is a general AI assistant. For writing within Notion, Notion AI is convenient. For general AI tasks, ChatGPT is more capable.

Can Notion AI summarize documents?

Yes, Notion AI can summarize selected text or entire pages. It works well for condensing meeting notes, articles, and lengthy documents.

Does Notion AI work offline?

Basic Notion works offline, but AI features require internet connectivity. AI commands won't work without an internet connection.

Can I use Notion AI for coding?

Notion AI has limited coding capabilities. It's primarily a writing and productivity assistant, not a coding tool. For coding, see Cursor or GitHub Copilot.

What's the difference between Notion and Notion AI?

Notion is the workspace tool (notes, databases, wikis). Notion AI is an add-on that brings AI assistance to Notion. You need Notion to use Notion AI.

Can teams use Notion AI?

Yes, the Business plan at $18/user/month includes team features with AI access. Teams using Notion for collaboration can benefit from shared AI assistance.
