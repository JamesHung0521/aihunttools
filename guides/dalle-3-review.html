What Is DALL-E 3?

DALL-E 3 is OpenAI's latest image generation model, released in late 2023. It builds on earlier DALL-E versions with significantly improved prompt following and integration with ChatGPT. Unlike Midjourney with its Discord-centric workflow, DALL-E 3 is designed for accessibility—generate images through ChatGPT, a clean web interface, or API.

The big improvement in DALL-E 3 is how well it interprets prompts. Previous AI image generators often ignored or misinterpreted parts of complex prompts. DALL-E 3 follows instructions more reliably, making it easier to get what you actually asked for.

Prompt Accuracy Test

This is where DALL-E 3 shines. I tested with increasingly complex prompts to see how well it follows instructions.

Simple Prompt

Prompt: "A red apple on a wooden table"

Perfect. Exactly what was requested, with realistic lighting and texture.

Medium Complexity

Prompt: "A golden retriever wearing sunglasses sitting in a red convertible, beach background, sunset lighting"

All elements present and recognizable. Dog, sunglasses, car, background, lighting—all accounted for.

High Complexity

Prompt: "A flat lay of a Mediterranean breakfast: feta cheese, olives, tomatoes, cucumber, spanakopita, Greek yogurt with honey, in the center a copy space with text reading 'BREAKFAST' in bold sans-serif font"

Impressive. Every food item was present, the composition was flat lay style, and the text was rendered correctly. Previous AI models would have struggled with this.

Style + Subject + Composition

Prompt: "A Renaissance oil painting style portrait of a cyberpunk mercenary, leather jacket with LED strips, robotic arm visible, moody chiaroscuro lighting, ornate frame"

DALL-E 3 handled the anachronistic combination well. The painting style was evident, the cyberpunk elements were present, lighting was dramatic. Not quite as artistically striking as Midjourney might produce, but accurate to the prompt.

Text Rendering Capabilities

Text in images was historically terrible with AI generators. DALL-E 3 dramatically improved this.

I tested:

  • "A sign that says HELLO WORLD" - Readable, correct
  • "A coffee cup with text reading 'COFFEE' in script font" - Mostly correct
  • "A storefront with 'OPEN' in neon lettering" - Correct
  • "A t-shirt with complex text 'AI FOR ALL'" - Mostly correct

Simple to medium text works reliably. Complex typography or longer text still has occasional errors, but it's vastly improved over DALL-E 2 and competitors.

ChatGPT Integration

DALL-E 3 is available directly within ChatGPT Plus. This integration is both a strength and limitation.

The good: You can describe what you want conversationally, iterate based on results, and use ChatGPT to help craft better prompts. If you're already paying for ChatGPT Plus ($20/month), you get DALL-E 3 access included.

The limitation: DALL-E 3 through ChatGPT has less control than standalone tools. You can't adjust aspect ratio, stylize strength, or use advanced parameters. For simple to medium complexity images, this doesn't matter. For precision work, you might prefer the API or other tools.

The standalone DALL-E 3 interface (labs.openai.com) offers more control—style presets (vivid/natural), aspect ratio, and quality settings.

Real-World Use Cases

Blog Post Illustrations

DALL-E 3 excels at creating illustrations for content. The prompt following means you get relevant images matching your specifications. For a tech blog post about cloud computing, I generated an appropriate illustration in seconds.

Social Media Graphics

With reliable text rendering, creating branded graphics is feasible. Design a template, generate with text, refine in Canva or Photoshop. The workflow is faster than starting from scratch.

Product Mockups

Drop products into scenes or create conceptual mockups. Quality is good for presentations and mood boards, though might not meet production photography standards.

Concept Art

DALL-E 3 handles concept art reasonably well, though it skews toward the safe and literal. Midjourney still wins for dramatic, artistic concept art with more creative interpretation.

Pricing and Credits

DALL-E 3 is included with ChatGPT Plus, which is the best value if you're already subscribed. Standalone credit purchases are reasonable for casual use or API integration.

Final Verdict

DALL-E 3 is the practical choice for most users. The prompt accuracy means you get what you ask for, the integration with ChatGPT is convenient, and the pricing is accessible to ChatGPT Plus subscribers.

But "practical" doesn't mean "best." For artistic work, dramatic compositions, and creative exploration, Midjourney still leads. For reliable, straightforward image generation with minimal friction, DALL-E 3 wins.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is DALL-E 3 free?

DALL-E 3 is included with ChatGPT Plus ($20/month). Standalone access requires purchasing credits or using the API. There's no permanently free tier.

Can DALL-E 3 edit existing images?

DALL-E 3 focuses on generation. For editing capabilities, look at tools like Midjourney's outpainting or dedicated editing tools built on Stable Diffusion.

How does DALL-E 3 compare to Midjourney?

See our detailed comparison. DALL-E 3 is better for prompt accuracy and ease of use; Midjourney is better for artistic quality and creative exploration.

What image resolutions does DALL-E 3 support?

DALL-E 3 generates 1024x1024 square images by default. Through the web interface, you can select landscape (1792x1024) or portrait (1024x1792) formats.

Can I use DALL-E 3 images commercially?

Yes, OpenAI grants full commercial rights to images created with DALL-E 3. You own the outputs you generate.

Why does DALL-E 3 refuse some requests?

DALL-E 3 has content safety filters preventing generation of explicit, violent, or harmful content, plus restrictions on generating images of real public figures without consent.

How many images can I generate?

With ChatGPT Plus, usage is limited but sufficient for most users. Exact limits aren't publicly disclosed and vary. API access is pay-per-generation.
