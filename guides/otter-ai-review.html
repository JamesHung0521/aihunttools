What Is Otter.ai?

Otter.ai is an AI-powered meeting transcription service. It listens to your meetings, transcribes the conversation in real-time, and provides summaries and action items automatically.

The pitch: stop taking notes manually. Otter attends your meetings, records audio, transcribes speech, and provides searchable, shareable transcripts.

I've used Otter.ai for weekly team meetings, client calls, and conference sessions. Here's my honest assessment.

Transcription Accuracy

Accuracy is the most important factor for any transcription service. Here's how Otter performs:

Ideal Conditions

With clear audio, good speakers, and minimal background noise, Otter achieves 90-95% accuracy. Technical terms and names are the main sources of errors.

I tested with a recorded technical discussion. Most conversation was transcribed accurately. "Kubernetes" became "kuber-knee-dees," "CI/CD" needed manual correction. But the overall meaning was preserved.

Challenging Conditions

With poor audio quality (conference calls, multiple speakers, background noise), accuracy drops. Multiple speakers talking over each other causes the most errors.

Speaker Identification

Otter identifies and labels speakers automatically. This works reasonably well when speakers have distinct voices. It struggles when multiple people have similar vocal characteristics.

Key Features

Real-Time Transcription

Otter transcribes as the meeting happens. You watch text appear in real-time. This is useful for live note-taking and ensuring nothing is missed.

Live Captions

Otter can display live captions during meetings. This is excellent for accessibility and for ensuring everyone can follow along, especially in large meetings or noisy environments.

Automatic Summaries

After the meeting, Otter generates an automatic summary with:

  • Key points discussed
  • Action items identified
  • Follow-up tasks assigned

The summaries are surprisingly good. They're not perfect, but they capture the main themes and often identify action items correctly.

Action Items

Otter identifies action items and assigns them to team members when it can determine who should do what. This is genuinely useful for follow-up.

Search and Playback

Transcripts are searchable. Click on any word to jump to that moment in the audio. This is excellent for finding specific discussions or decisions.

Collaboration

Share transcripts with meeting attendees and others. Add comments, highlight sections, and collaborate on action items.

Platform Integrations

Otter integrates with popular meeting platforms:

  • Zoom: Otter joins Zoom meetings automatically, transcribes in real-time
  • Google Meet: Via browser extension, Otter can transcribe Google Meet calls
  • Microsoft Teams: Integration available for Teams meetings
  • Web conferencing: OtterPilot feature auto-joins and transcribes meetings

The Zoom integration is particularly smooth. If you schedule meetings through Zoom and connect calendars, Otter can automatically join and transcribe.

Real-World Testing

Weekly Team Standup

Format: 30-minute video call with 6 team members

Accuracy: ~90%. Action items identified correctly. Summary captured main updates.

Client Discovery Call

Format: 60-minute call with client and two team members

Accuracy: ~85%. Client's industry terminology caused some errors. But the overall transcript was valuable for creating follow-up documents.

Conference Session Recording

Format: Pre-recorded video of a technical talk

Accuracy: ~95%. Clear audio from a single speaker produced excellent results.

Pricing Breakdown

Plan Price Key Features
Free $0 300 minutes/month, 3 meetings, limited exports
Pro $20/month 6000 minutes/month, unlimited meetings, advanced exports
Business $30/user/month Unlimited transcription, admin controls, SSO, analytics

The free tier is limited (300 minutes, 3 meetings per month) but good for trying the service. Pro at $20/month is reasonable for individuals with regular meetings. Business is for teams needing organization-wide deployment.

Final Verdict

Otter.ai genuinely improves meeting productivity. The transcription is accurate enough to be useful, the summaries save time, and the integrations make it easy to adopt.

It's not perfect—transcription errors happen, especially with poor audio—but the overall value is real. If you attend lots of meetings and struggle to take notes, Otter solves that problem.

For teams, the Business plan's organization-wide visibility and admin controls make sense. For individuals, Pro is usually sufficient.

My rating: 8.2/10. Solid tool that delivers on its promise.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Otter.ai accurate?

Otter achieves 90-95% accuracy under good audio conditions. Accuracy drops with poor audio, multiple speakers talking over each other, or heavy accents. It's accurate enough to be useful but not perfect.

Can Otter transcribe pre-recorded audio?

Yes, you can upload audio or video files for transcription. This is useful for transcribing recordings, podcasts, or interviews.

Does Otter work with Zoom?

Yes, Otter integrates with Zoom. It can auto-join scheduled meetings, transcribe in real-time, and sync with your Zoom recordings.

Is Otter.ai private and secure?

Otter uses encryption and offers enterprise-grade security. However, you're uploading audio to their servers for transcription. Review their privacy policy for sensitive content.

Can I edit transcripts?

Yes, you can edit transcripts after the meeting. This includes correcting transcription errors, adding speaker names, and highlighting important sections.

What's the difference between Otter and OtterPilot?

OtterPilot is Otter's feature that automatically joins and transcribes your meetings. It's available on Pro and Business plans and handles scheduling and joining automatically.

Can Otter translate transcripts?

Otter transcribes in the language spoken. For translation, you'd need a separate translation service. Some integrations support translation.
